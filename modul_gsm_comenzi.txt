* pentru a apela un numar de telefon: - ATD07xxxxxxxx; and to end the call ATH

* pentru a trimite un sms : AT+CMGF=1 --- Set SMS Mode to Text:
                           AT+CMGS="07xxxxxxxx" 
                           scrie mesaj si apasa ctrl+z
* pentru a citi mesaje sms: AT+CMGL="ALL"

* pentru a vedea puterea semnalului : AT+CSQ

* to check the available network cells: AT+CNETSCAN
* To list available cellular networks (cells) :  AT+COPS=?
* The command “AT+CENG” is designed to put the unit into “Engineering Mode”. AT+CENG=2,1
+CENG: <index>, <Cell ID>, <Signal Strength>, <LAC>, <MCC>, <MNC>, <ARFCN>
